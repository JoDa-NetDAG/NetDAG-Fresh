NetDAG Ambassadors

Ambassador Program

The NetDAG Ambassador Program is a relationship, not a paid shill gig. If you genuinely believe in building a math-backed, real-utility crypto economy and want to help explain it to others, we would love to hear from you.

Who this is for

We welcome people who already add value to their communities:

  • Content creators (YouTube, TikTok, X, long-form writers, podcasters).
  • Community builders (Telegram/Discord admins, meetup organizers, local leaders).
  • Educators and explainers who enjoy turning complex topics into clear language.
  • Researchers / DeFi and infra nerds who want to test and stress-test our ideas.
No pump-and-dump No fake promises Long-term mindset

What NetDAG Ambassadors do

  • Teach & explain: create honest content about NetDAG, NDG, the bonding curve, Guardian, dVPN, and provenance.
  • Host conversations: AMAs, spaces, meetups or private sessions for your local community.
  • Give feedback: share what your audience actually understands, likes, or worries about.
  • Spot partnerships: introduce projects, companies or communities that could benefit from NetDAG rails.
  • Protect the vibe: help keep our ecosystem away from hype, scams, and unrealistic expectations.
Ambassadorship is always opt-in and reversible. If at any point you feel misaligned with our direction, we prefer an honest conversation over forced branding.

How it works

  1. Introduce yourself: send us a short message or email with links to your work and communities.
  2. We talk: if there is a good fit, we schedule a call or async chat to align on values and expectations.
  3. Trial phase: we start with a small, low-pressure collaboration (content pieces, events, or experiments).
  4. Ongoing role: if it works for both sides, we define a longer-term ambassador path with clear goals.
Ambassadorship is always opt-in and reversible. If at any point you feel misaligned with our direction, we prefer an honest conversation over forced branding.

What we look for

There is no minimum follower count. We care more about alignment and integrity:

  • You are comfortable saying "I don't know" instead of inventing answers.
  • You are willing to highlight risks as well as opportunities.
  • You care about users more than short-term price action.
  • You are okay rejecting shady offers, even if they pay more.

What ambassadors can receive

  • NDG-denominated rewards tied to real impact (not pure follower numbers).
  • Early access to features, documents, and discussions.
  • Direct communication with the core contributors.
  • Visibility on the website and official channels where appropriate.
Rewards are always transparent and capped. We do not sell private allocations or guaranteed returns. Ambassadors are partners, not insiders.

Quick questions

Is this a paid job?

No. It is a flexible collaboration. We may reward contributions, but we do not hire people to "say only nice things" or to manufacture hype.

Do I need to be a DeFi expert?

Basic crypto literacy helps, but you do not need to be a solidity engineer. We can support you with materials, diagrams, and explanations.

Can I be an ambassador for other projects too?

Yes, as long as there is no conflict of interest and you stay transparent. We will decline collaborations that promote obvious scams or pump-and-dump schemes.

How do I apply?

Use the contact options below. Share who you are, what you already do, and how you imagine contributing. Short and honest is better than a long sales pitch.
