Charity by Design

A protocol-level giving model where a fixed share of NetDAG's real-world usage flows on-chain to social impact — transparently and continuously.

The Charity-First Model

Philanthropy isn't an afterthought in NetDAG — it's embedded in code. Our protocol routes a fixed share of network revenue into a dedicated on-chain Charity Pool, making social good a continuous, measurable outcome of ecosystem activity.

Key idea: Every time someone uses NetDAG — dVPN sessions, Provenance scans, or future apps — a small portion contributes to the Charity Pool automatically.

How It Works: The 2% Contribution Model

  • Protocol Level: 2% of eligible revenue/fees is routed to the Charity Pool by smart contracts.
  • Continuous Flow: Not a one-off donation — an always-on, usage-driven mechanism.
  • Backed by Math: Amounts, timing, and recipients are fully auditable on-chain.
  • dVPN: A portion of per-minute/GB fees channels into the pool.
  • Provenance: Each verification fee contributes a small share.
  • Future dApps: New modules can opt-in to the same giving policy.

Exact fee splits per product are disclosed in contracts and dashboards at launch.

Transparency and Trust, On-Chain

  • Traceable Donations: Pool inflows and outflows are recorded on-chain.
  • Public Reporting: Periodic summaries of amounts, recipients, and impact KPIs.
  • Community Governance: The DAO approves recipient lists and disbursement schedules.
  • Compliance: Partner charities complete verification to qualify for on-chain disbursements.

Turning Commerce into Contribution

Businesses

Using NetDAG Provenance? Part of each verification fee supports vetted causes, creating brand goodwill and measurable impact.

Everyday Users

Browsing privately on dVPN? Your session contributes a fraction to the Charity Pool — privacy with purpose.

Over time, predictable contributions compound into substantial, verifiable impact — the flywheel of utility → liquidity → social good.

Get Involved

  • Suggest a Cause: Propose charities for the DAO shortlist via our community channels.
  • Track the Pool: Follow inflows/outflows on public dashboards once live.
  • Become a Partner: NGOs and foundations can contact us to join the verified recipients list.

For partnership and verification inquiries, email charity@netdag.com.
