Core mechanics & buying NDG

What is the NetDAG Bonding Curve?

It is a mathematical pricing curve where NDG price is linked to circulating supply. Buys move the price smoothly up the curve and burns move it back down, helping keep liquidity predictable instead of spiky.

What happens after I buy NDG?

After your payment is confirmed, the smart contract sends NDG directly to the wallet address you used for the purchase. You see a transaction confirmation and the tokens are immediately secured on the blockchain.

How do I receive the NDG I paid for?

You connect your wallet before buying. Once the transaction is mined, the contract delivers your NDG to that same wallet automatically — no manual claim, no waiting and no intermediaries.

When is the presale and how does it work?

The presale is planned for early February 2026. The exact date will be announced soon. During the presale, a limited supply of NDG will be sold along the NetDAG Bonding Curve — meaning early participants enter at lower prices while a portion of every purchase automatically strengthens long-term backing and liquidity.

How does NDG burning work?

NDG burns come from three sources: voluntary user burns, 10% protocol revenue buy-back & burn, and quarterly treasury burns.

How much NDG is burned?

NetDAG burns 10% of ecosystem revenue and up to 1% of treasury-held NDG every quarter.

Why does NetDAG burn tokens?

To increase scarcity over time, align NDG value with real platform usage, and support a sustainable deflation curve.

Guardian, dVPN, Provenance & safety

What is the NetDAG Guardian (AI layer)?

Guardian is an AI-assisted risk layer that watches on-chain data and market conditions. It can trigger or suggest predefined safety rules to protect liquidity and protocol health instead of relying only on human reactions.

How does the decentralised VPN (dVPN) work?

The dVPN turns community bandwidth into a privacy network. Nodes relay encrypted traffic and earn rewards, while users route their data without trusting a single central provider.

What is the NetDAG Provenance dApp?

It lets brands and creators bind products to verifiable on-chain IDs using barcodes, QR or NFC tags. Scanning the code shows origin, authenticity and ownership history.

How does NetDAG approach security?

Contracts are designed around battle-tested DeFi patterns: audits, multi-sig treasuries and clear separation between user funds and protocol fees. The Guardian layer also monitors for unusual behaviour and can activate safeguards.

Is NetDAG compliant with regulations?

NetDAG focuses on infrastructure and utility, not guaranteed returns. The team works with legal advisors and aims to follow evolving guidance on token classification, AML/KYC for on- and off-ramps, and consumer protection. Details still depend on each user's country.

How does the charity or social-impact model work?

A transparent share of protocol revenue is planned for a dedicated charity wallet. The community can help choose causes and all distributions are traceable on-chain.

How can I get involved or get help?

You can join as a community member, builder, node operator or partner. Use the official links on the site, read the whitepaper and roadmap, and contact us if you have questions.
