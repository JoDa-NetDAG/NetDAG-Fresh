Core mechanics, presale & NDG

What problem does NetDAG solve?

NetDAG removes transaction friction through a gasless-first user experience while enabling trusted product provenance (anti-counterfeit) and secure blockchain payments.

What is the NetDAG bonding curve?
It’s a mathematical pricing mechanism where NDG price relates to circulating supply. Buys move price smoothly up the curve, and sells/burns move it down—designed to keep liquidity more predictable than thin order books.
What happens after I buy NDG?
After your transaction is confirmed, NDG is delivered to the wallet address used in the purchase. You can verify delivery on-chain via the transaction hash.
How do I receive the NDG I paid for?
You connect a wallet before buying. Once the transaction is mined, the contract transfers NDG to that same wallet automatically—no manual claim process.
When is the presale and how does it work?
The presale is planned for early 2026 (see the ticker for current messaging). NDG is sold along the bonding curve: early participants enter at lower prices, and purchases contribute to long-term backing and liquidity.
How do burns work and why burn NDG?
Burns are intended to reduce supply over time and align NDG value with real platform usage.
  • Protocol buy-back & burn: a share of ecosystem revenue can be used to buy NDG and burn it (when enabled by policy).
  • Treasury burns: periodic burns may occur depending on governance decisions and treasury health.
  • Voluntary burns: users may burn NDG in certain flows (if supported).
Is staking available, and what does staking do?
Staking is designed to reward long-term participation and can support network health (depending on implementation). If/when staking is enabled, always use official links and verify contract addresses before depositing.
Is NetDAG a stablecoin?
No. NetDAG is building a stability-first ecosystem, but NDG is still a crypto asset and can fluctuate. The goal is to reduce fragility and panic dynamics—not to promise a fixed price.

Guardian, Easy Access, dVPN, Provenance & safety

What is Guardian AI (and what is it not)?
Guardian is an AI-assisted risk intelligence layer. It focuses on detecting unhealthy patterns (concentration, liquidity drains, exploit signals). It is not “a promise of profit” and not a magic price-control tool.
What does “gas‑free” mean in Easy Access?
“Gas‑free” means the user experience can show $0 gas when transactions are sponsored (for example by an app paymaster). Under the hood, transactions still happen on-chain—fees are simply handled in the background.
Do I need a wallet or seed phrase to use NetDAG?
Not always. Easy Access is designed so users can onboard with familiar sign-in methods. Advanced users can still use standard wallets. Recovery options depend on the onboarding method you choose.
How does the decentralised VPN (dVPN) work?
The dVPN turns community bandwidth into a privacy network. Nodes relay encrypted traffic and can earn rewards, while users route data without trusting a single central provider.
What is the Provenance dApp?
Provenance lets brands and creators bind products to verifiable on-chain IDs via QR/NFC. Scanning shows authenticity signals, origin, and (optionally) ownership or certification history.
How does NetDAG approach security?
NetDAG aims to use standard security practices: audits where appropriate, multi-sig custody for treasuries, careful permission boundaries, and continuous monitoring. Always verify domains, links, and contract addresses from official sources.
How do I avoid scams and fake NDG links?
  • Only use links from your official site and verified socials.
  • Never share seed phrases or private keys—no admin will ask.
  • Double-check token contract addresses and transaction prompts.
  • If something feels rushed (“limited time, send now”), treat it as suspicious.
Is NetDAG compliant with regulations?
NetDAG focuses on infrastructure and utility, not guaranteed returns. Requirements vary by country. For any on/off-ramps or partner services, compliance (including AML/KYC) may apply depending on jurisdiction.
How does the charity / social-impact model work?
A transparent share of protocol revenue may be allocated to a dedicated charity wallet. The community can help choose causes, and distributions can be tracked on-chain.
How can I get involved or get help?
Join as a community member, builder, node operator, or partner. Use the official links on the site, read the Whitepaper/Roadmap, and use the Contact form for questions.
