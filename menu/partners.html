Partners & Integrations

  • AI Stability Layer: The NetDAG Guardian monitors activity and proposes optimizations.

Partner Tracks

Technology

  • Listings, custody, staking/vesting hooks.

  • Co-development pilots and research.

Enterprise & Brands

Supply chain, retail, pharma, luxury, food, and logistics teams looking for verifiable provenance.

NGOs & Foundations

  • Charity pool disbursements with public addresses.

How Integration Works

  1. Discovery: Define goals, data flows, and milestones together.
  2. Technical Plan: Select SDKs/APIs, authentication, and sandbox or testnet access.
  3. Pilot: Run a limited-scope rollout with clear metrics and reporting.
  4. Scale: Harden, optimize, and expand to new regions, products, or user segments.
Security & Compliance: Key integrations go through security review, on-chain audits (where relevant), and governance sign-off for sensitive parameter changes. We prefer careful growth over rushed deployments.
