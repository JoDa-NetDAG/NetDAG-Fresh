NetDAG Roadmap

From foundations and presale to dVPN, provenance, Guardian upgrades and long-term governance — a stability-first path instead of hype dates.

NetDAG Roadmap

NetDAG is a systemic DeFi / RWA project built on three pillars: a financial engine (bonding-curve shock absorber), an AI brain (Neuron / Guardian), and a physical dApp layer (provenance + retail integrations). This roadmap is not a hype schedule; it is a sequence of real milestones that connect those three pillars into one working system.

Phase 0 · Foundations

Concept, math & architecture

Status: In motion

  • Define NetDAG's core mission: stability-first, real-world-linked crypto economy.
  • Formalise the three core pillars: Financial Engine, Neuron AI, Provenance dApp.
  • Design bonding-curve parameters and 22% reserve "shock absorber".
  • Mathematically validate the reserve + curve model and prepare partner-facing docs.
  • High-level architecture for Neuron AI, oracle feeds and provenance / RWA flows.
  • Brand identity, web presence and documentation structure (whitepaper, tokenomics).

Phase 1 · Launch

Presale, core contracts & first guardian

Status: Upcoming

  • Deploy NDG token, bonding-curve reserve and timelock / treasury contracts on BNB Chain.
  • Lock the permanent reserve on-chain and publish verifiable addresses and dashboards.
  • Run the initial presale with transparent curve-based price steps and supply caps.
  • Integrate oracles and monitoring so Neuron / Guardian can observe market conditions.
  • Launch Guardian (beta) in advisory mode with public risk and liquidity signals.
  • List NDG on the first DEX and enable curve-aware buy-back and arbitrage incentives.

Phase 2 · Network utilities

dVPN & provenance / RWA dApp

Status: Planned

  • Release the first NetDAG dVPN client & node stack with NDG-denominated bandwidth.
  • Launch the provenance dApp for verifying digital media and physical products.
  • Align the dApp design with existing standards (barcodes, QR, NFC) for easy adoption.
  • Pipe usage metrics (scans, sessions, nodes) into Neuron / Guardian for live analytics.
  • Create SDKs and docs so external teams can build on dVPN and provenance rails.
  • Experiment with NDG incentives for early users, node operators and verifiers.

Phase 3 · Ecosystem & commerce

Real-world integration & loyalty

Status: Planned

  • Open affiliate and commerce bridges so Web2 / Web3 partners can plug into NetDAG.
  • Structure pilots where brands pay simple per-scan or per-session fees (no token risk).
  • Convert a portion of that real-world revenue into NDG buy-backs on the bonding curve.
  • Introduce staking and loyalty programs tied to verifiable activity, not pure speculation.
  • Launch community-led ecosystem projects that extend the dVPN and provenance stack.

Phase 4 · Guardian upgrade

From advisory to active defence

Status: Future

  • Upgrade Guardian / Neuron from "read-only advisor" to carefully-limited on-chain actor.
  • Allow governance to tune risk thresholds, intervention rules and reserve usage policies.
  • Deploy more sophisticated anomaly detection and circuit-breaker style protections.
  • Expose full transparency dashboards for every Guardian action and outcome.
  • Offer opt-in user profiles (conservative / neutral / adventurous) for strategy preferences.

Phase 5 · Governance & autonomy

NetDAG as self-sustaining infrastructure

Status: Vision

  • Progressively hand control of key parameters to on-chain governance and the community.
  • Formalise NDG as the coordination asset across all NetDAG products and revenue flows.
  • Extend multi-chain support and cross-chain liquidity rails for the bonding-curve engine.
  • Grow a network of independent teams and partners building specialised apps on NetDAG.
  • Ensure long-term funding for public goods, audits and ecosystem maintenance.

How to read this roadmap

Phases are sequenced, not calendar-locked. Some will overlap, some may be reordered by audits, regulation or partner feedback. We prefer to ship quietly and report honestly rather than publishing aggressive date promises.

Guiding principles

Every step we take must respect these guardrails:

Stability before speculation On-chain transparency Security over speed Real usage > fake volume RWA & partner-friendly design Community alignment

Partnership lens

NetDAG's ecosystem is built around three partner types: financial allies who understand the reserve and curve, AI / data infrastructure that Neuron can rely on, and real-world brands and manufacturers that care about provenance and anti-counterfeit. This roadmap keeps all three in view.

NetDAG is deliberately patient: the goal is to build infrastructure that can survive hype cycles, not chase them. If you are here only for a quick flip, this roadmap will probably feel too slow — and that is by design.

A visual timeline graphic will be added here later, once the first contracts, audits and pilot partnerships are live. Until then, this text is the canonical roadmap.
