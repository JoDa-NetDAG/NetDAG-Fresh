🏦 Treasury Management (Unsold Tokens)

Post-Presale Redistribution Plan

Any unsold tokens after the 9-month presale period will be transferred to the Treasury and redistributed according to this strategic plan to support long-term ecosystem operations and allocation discipline.

🛡️ Bonding Curve Reserve 20%
💎 Staking Rewards Pool 10%
⚡ Gas-Free Transaction Pool 10%
🔧 Treasury Operations 10%
🔥 Strategic Reserve (Resale or Burn) 50%
⏰ Timeline:
  • Months 1-9: Active presale period (9 tiers)
  • Month 10: Optional CEO extension (up to 12 months total)
  • Months 10-12: 50% strategic reserve can be resold (max 3 months)
  • After Month 12: All remaining unsold tokens MUST be burned
✅ Transparency Commitment:
All Treasury actions will be publicly announced and permanently verifiable on-chain via BSCScan.
