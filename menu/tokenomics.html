NDG Burn Mechanism — Balanced Deflation Model

The NDG token uses a sustainable deflation model designed to support long-term value while ensuring liquidity and ecosystem stability.

1) Voluntary Holder Burns

Any NDG holder may burn their own tokens at any time via the on-chain burn() function. Burns are optional and never required.

2) Revenue Buy-Back & Burn (10%)

NetDAG allocates 10% of all ecosystem revenue toward buying NDG on the open market and burning it permanently.

3) Treasury Burn (1% Quarterly)

The NetDAG Treasury may burn up to 1% of its NDG holdings every quarter.

4) Transparency

All burns are announced publicly and permanently verifiable on-chain.
