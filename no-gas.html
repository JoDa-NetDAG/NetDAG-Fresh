Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this really free forever?

A: Yes. We've allocated 3% of our 1 billion token supply (30 million NDG) specifically for gas sponsorship. At current BNB prices, this funds millions of transactions. It's a permanent commitment, not a temporary promotion.

Q: Can NetDAG access my funds?

A: No. Your wallet is non-custodial—only you can authorize transactions. NetDAG simply sponsors the gas fees using Biconomy Paymaster technology. We never touch your tokens.

Q: What if I want to use MetaMask instead?

A: You can! NetDAG supports both gasless accounts AND traditional wallets like MetaMask. Power users can choose their preferred method, while newcomers enjoy the simple experience.
