How NetDAG Provenance Works

A simple verification flow for brands, manufacturers, buyers, and public product checks.

1

Manufacturer creates a product record

Product details such as name, batch, origin, serial number, issuer, and shipment route are entered into the Provenance app.

2

Record is anchored on-chain

The product record is converted into a verification hash and anchored as tamper-resistant proof.

3

QR code is attached to the product

The product receives a unique QR code that links directly to its public NetDAG Provenance certificate.

4

Customer scans the QR code

A buyer scans the product with a phone before purchase, resale, delivery, warranty, or inspection.

5

NetDAG verifies authenticity

The app checks whether the Product ID exists as a confirmed on-chain Provenance record.

6

Guardian Confidence is displayed

Verified records show confidence context, helping users quickly understand whether the product proof looks strong.

Verify shoes, apparel, limited editions, and branded products before purchase or resale.

Luxury Watches & Jewellery

Electronics & Mobile Devices

Confirm product identity, batch origin, warranty status, and distribution history.

Luxury Goods & Handbags

Protect premium bags, accessories, and collectibles from counterfeit circulation.

Automotive Components

Help buyers and workshops verify spare parts, safety components, and supply-chain origin.

Pharmaceutical Products

Support verification of packaging, batches, and certified product movement.

Food & Agricultural Products

Verify the origin, harvest location, processing history, and authenticity of products such as honey, coffee, cocoa, olive oil, spices, and premium farm goods.

Premium Consumer Goods

Verify perfumes, cosmetics, wines, coffee, and other premium retail products.

Art & Collectibles

Attach verifiable proof to rare items, certificates, ownership history, and authenticity records.

Digital Assets

Support verification of tokenized items, digital certificates, ownership proofs, and asset-linked authenticity records.

Toys & Collectibles

Help verify limited-edition toys, collectibles, sealed products, and branded merchandise before resale or purchase.
